What The Calculator Does

This calculator calculates growth rate of bacteria and things like initial/final populations and elapsed time based on the growth rate. It will also calculate either the doubling time or half-life of the bacteria depending on if the growth rate is positive or negetive. The calculator will solve for the data requested and display a completed set of data in table.

  • Growth Rate: Rate at which a population either grows or decays (negetive growth rate)
  • Doubling Time: Time it takes for a population to double
  • Half-Life: Time it takes for a population to half
  • Initial/Final Population: Population counts at two different points in time

While the purpose of this program is to calculate details for bacteria, it can really be used in any case where there is exponential growth.
